For the real estate entrepreneur, or potential home buyer, Dream Real Estate is a product that that will provide a streamlined approach to looking for homes, and those home’s overall safety. 
The application will provide users with visual representation of the value shifts of a home, work done on the home, and other information about the history of the home. This application will provide real estate agents with niche 
data to better server home buyers, and home buyers with a tool to better inform themselves. Unlike todays modern apps, such as Zillow, this product will offer data on the prevalence of crime that surrounds the home. 
This currently rare, if not non-existent feature, will give users insight to better protect themselves and their families by mitigating exposure to the type of crime they’re most vulnerable too.
